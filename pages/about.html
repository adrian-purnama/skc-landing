About Us

Usmber Kreasi Cemerlang, established in 2001, is a trusted provider specializing in signage, visual branding, and creative fabrication solutions. With over two decades of experience and a growing production facility in Banten, we deliver design, engineering, fabrication, and installation services across Indonesia. Our growth is driven by innovation, precision, and a strong customer-oriented approach, positioning Usmber Kreasi Cemerlang as a reliable partner for businesses seeking impactful and enduring brand visibility.

SPACE

SPACE represents our fundamental principles that guide everything we do: Safety - Ensuring the highest standards of safety in all our operations, Professionalism - Maintaining excellence and integrity in every project, Accountability - Taking responsibility for our actions and results, Collaboration - Working together with respect and shared goals, Excellence - Committed to delivering the highest quality solutions.

Safety

Professionalism

Accountability

Taking responsibility for our actions and results, ensuring transparency and reliability in all our business processes and commitments to stakeholders

Collaboration

Excellence

Our Coverage

Our Aftersales Coverage ensures that every product we deliver is supported with reliable, responsive, and comprehensive service. Our nationwide service network and skilled technicians are always ready to assist, providing peace of mind long after the initial purchase.

We are also committed to building the manufacturing industry in Indonesia by collaborating with local companies, building their industrial capabilities and benefiting the local economy.
